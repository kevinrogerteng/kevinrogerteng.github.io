Grokkie

Description: Grokkie is a web application to help users remember and share the resources they have used to 'grok' (or in other words, truly understand) a certain subject. A collection of these resources is known as a 'Roadmap.' Roadmaps are a clear route to grokking a certain skill, with each resource being a stop along the way. Grokkie was a group project and developed by 4 individuals.

Inspiration: One thing we did NOT have in common as a group was our different learning styles, some of us preferred to learn on our own by reading various articles on programmers while others prefer watching youtube videos and coding along. We wanted to share our knowledge of learning Ruby, Rails and Javascript using various resources and approaches (that we think is best).

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, Postgres, Yelp Api, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery UI, Bitly Api, Paperclip

What's next? Revamp group project and using Angular JS

Feedback First

Description: Feedback First is an API built for an existing customer support application. Feedback First allows you to create reports or posts based on tags on customer feedback, this creates a more meaningful and supported stand when requesting to add or improve an app feature.

Inspiration: While I was working as a Customer Service Representative, I noticed how isolated our department is in terms of interactivity and involvement. I wanted to make the Support department more involved with the Product Management department's decision in adding or improving a new feature by making use of customer feedback.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, Postgres, Angular JS, HTML5, CSS3

What's next? Editing graph, adding more than one graph and Re-styling progress bars
